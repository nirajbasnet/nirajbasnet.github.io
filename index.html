About Me

Roboticist by heart, I am passionate about building autonomous intelligent systems. I am a recent Master's graduate in Computer Science from Oregon State University. With 2 years of industry experience and 5+ years of multi-disciplinary academic experience in various aspects of autonomous systems, I have garnered hands-on knowledge across different robotic platforms (aerial vehicles, autonomous cars, humanoid robots) and domains (motion planning, control, state estimation). My research experience focuses on the planning, control, and verification of autonomous systems. Recently, I was involved in building planning and control stack for OSU team for high-speed racing of F1/10 cars for the annual F1/10 racing competition.
